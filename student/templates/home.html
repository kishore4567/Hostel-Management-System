About

Kongu Engineering College (KEC), located in the vibrant city of Erode, Tamil Nadu, is one of Indiaâ€™s premier institutions for higher education. Established in 1984, the college is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, excellent academic track record, and a commitment to holistic student development. Affiliated with Anna University, KEC offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, and management.
